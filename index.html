Advanced Computer Vision Models

Our proprietary AI models extract actionable insights from billboard camera feeds with industry-leading accuracy.

Gaze Detection & Engagement Tracking

Measures precise impression data including duration of customer engagements, dwell time, and attention patterns. Our models can distinguish between casual glances and meaningful engagement.

Key Metrics:

Attention Duration Dwell Time Engagement Score Repeat Views

Vehicle & Demographic Analysis

Uses object detection to track vehicle types, counts, and approximate demographics. Our models can classify vehicles by make/model/year and correlate with demographic data.

Key Metrics:

Vehicle Count Make/Model Demographic Proxy Traffic Patterns

License Plate Recognition

Advanced scene text detection and recognition to extract license plate data (where legally permitted) for measuring repeat exposure and journey mapping.

Key Metrics:

Unique Vehicles Repeat Exposure Journey Mapping Market Penetration

Environmental & Seasonal Tracking

Monitors weather patterns, lighting conditions, and seasonal trends per structure location to optimize campaign timing and measure environmental impact on performance.

Key Metrics:

Weather Impact Lighting Conditions Seasonal Trends Time-of-Day Analysis

Proof of Posting (PoP) Verification

Vision Language Model quality control alerts for damaged or improperly deployed vinyls, ensuring campaign integrity and activating maintenance workflows.

Key Metrics:

Vinyl Integrity Proper Deployment Damage Detection Maintenance Alerts

Audience Measurement

Privacy-compliant demographic analysis estimating age ranges, gender distribution, and group dynamics of audiences engaging with each billboard.

Age Distribution Gender Mix Group Dynamics Foot Traffic
