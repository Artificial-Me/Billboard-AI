Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Billboard AI.

Our hardware devices are equipped with high-resolution cameras and environmental sensors that capture anonymous audience data. Using edge computing, the devices process images locally to extract demographic information (like approximate age and gender) and engagement metrics (like dwell time) without storing personally identifiable information. The processed data is then securely transmitted to our cloud platform for analysis and reporting.

Installation is quick and straightforward. Our devices are designed to mount on most standard billboard structures with minimal modification. The entire process typically takes less than 2 hours per billboard. We provide detailed installation guides and video tutorials, and our support team is available to assist with any questions. For larger deployments, we can arrange professional installation services.

Privacy is our top priority. Our system is designed from the ground up to comply with all relevant privacy regulations including GDPR and CCPA. We never capture or store personally identifiable information. All image processing happens locally on the device, and only aggregated, anonymized data is transmitted to our servers. We also provide tools for advertisers to display privacy notices and opt-out information near monitored billboards.

Professional and Enterprise plans add priority phone support and dedicated account management.
